I am Shadan Golestan, a postdoctoral fellow at the Computing Science of the University of Alberta, and Alberta Machine Intelligence Institute (Amii) working with Prof. Osmar Zaiane.

The figure below shows the main themes of my research: 1: Domain Knowledge Integration via Data-driven Learning, 2: Learning Representations and Synthetic Dataset Generation, and 3: Desicion-making Agents. I focus on designing agents via Reinforcement Learning, Bayesian Optimization, and Large Language Models, to solve sequential decision-making problems, such as system optimization and machine/robot adaptation techniques in real-world applications.

Before that, I was a PhD candidate in the same department under the supervision of Prof. Pierre Boulanger. I researched on Bayesian optimization and simulation methodologies for optimizing and evaluating smart indoor spaces.
